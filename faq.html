Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sign up?

You can sign up by visiting our Signup Page. Fill in the required details, and you'll be ready to explore our website!

2. What categories of books are available?

We offer a wide range of categories, including fiction, non-fiction, mystery, romance, self-help, and more. Visit our Home Page and then Categories section to explore.

3. How do the Amazon links work?

Each book on our site has an Amazon link, allowing you to purchase the book directly from Amazon. Simply click on the link, and you'll be redirected to the product page on Amazon.

4. Can I read reviews before purchasing a book?

Yes! Our Reviews Page contains detailed reviews from other readers, helping you make an informed decision before purchasing.

5. How does the resell feature work?

Our Resell Page allows you to list books you want to sell and browse books resold by other users. It's a great way to give your books a second life or find affordable options.

6. How can I add books to my cart?

When you find a book you like, like on the Cart . You can view and manage your cart anytime from the cart icon in the navigation bar.
